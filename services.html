Frequently Asked Questions

How do I schedule a pickup?

You can schedule a pickup through our website or by calling our customer service line. We offer flexible time slots to accommodate your schedule.

What is your turnaround time?

Our standard turnaround time is 24-48 hours for Wash & Fold service and 48-72 hours for Dry Cleaning. We also offer express service for an additional fee.

Do you offer same-day service?

Yes, we offer same-day service for orders placed before 9 AM, with delivery by 7 PM. This service is available for an additional fee and is subject to availability.

What areas do you serve?

We currently serve the Ghaziabad area and surrounding suburbs within a 15-mile radius of our main facility.
